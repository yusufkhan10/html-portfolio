Top 3 movies of all time (according to Yusuf)

Looking for a movie to watch? Look no further.

12 Angry Men (1957): Sidney Lumet

Taking place only in a single jury room, a jury discusses the outcome of a young boy accused of murdering his father. While simple in every perceivable way, '12 Angry Men's subtle thematic elements and metaphors elevate the film to beautiful proportions, and is a true embodiment of justice.

Back to the Future: Robert Zemeckis (1985)

Another classic, 'Back to the Future' features everything you could possibly want in a good movie. With stellar performances, hilarious writing, and intense action, Back to the Future has something for everyone.

Prisoners (2013): Denis Villeneuve

A gruelling thriller, 'Prisoners' follows a father after his daughter and her friend were kidnapped, and the efforts of a police officer with a perfect record to track them down. With such a volatile and intense search, you never know what will happen next.
